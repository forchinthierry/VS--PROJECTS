2. Eligibility

To apply for a loan with G-Spirit Loan Solution, you must:

  • Be at least 21 years old
  • Be a citizen or legal resident of Cameroon
  • Have a valid government-issued ID
  • Provide proof of residence (utility bill, rent receipt, etc.)
  • Have a verifiable source of income
  • Own acceptable collateral that can be physically verified
  • Have an active mobile money account (MTN or Orange) or bank account

3. Loan Terms

3.1 Interest Rate

All loans carry a fixed interest rate of 10% per month, calculated on the principal amount for the duration of the loan.

3.2 Processing Fee

A one-time processing fee of 10% of the loan amount is deducted upfront, before funds are disbursed.

3.3 Repayment

Loans must be repaid by the agreed date. Repaying early is encouraged and carries no penalty — if you settle a loan ahead of schedule, interest is only charged for the period the funds were actually in use.

4. Collateral Requirements

Every loan requires physical collateral that our team can verify in person. We accept:

  • Land titles — must be registered and free of any disputes
  • Vehicle registration papers — cars, motorcycles, or trucks in good condition
  • Business equipment — machinery, electronics, or tools, valued based on condition
  • Property deeds — houses or commercial properties
  • Other valuable assets — high-value electronics, appliances, or inventory

Collateral must be worth at least 1.5 times the loan amount. A physical inspection is carried out before any loan is approved.

5. Late Payments and Default

5.1 Late Payment Penalty

A 5% daily penalty applies to any outstanding balance not repaid by the due date. This penalty continues to accrue until the full amount is settled.

5.2 Grace Period

A 3-day grace period follows the due date, during which no extra penalties apply. We encourage borrowers to reach out during this window to discuss extension options.

5.3 Default

If a loan remains unpaid 15 days past its due date, it is considered in default. In this case:

  • Your collateral may be seized and sold to recover the debt
  • Legal action may be taken to recover the outstanding amount
  • Your information may be shared with credit bureaus
  • Collection costs may be added to your balance

6. Loan Extension and Restructuring

If you expect trouble repaying on time, contact us before the due date. Depending on your situation, we may offer:

  • Extension — extending your loan term, with adjusted interest
  • Partial payment — paying part now and extending the remainder
  • Restructuring — a new payment plan tailored to your circumstances

Extension fees may apply — reach out to discuss what works for you.
