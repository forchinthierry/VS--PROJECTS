Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

1. Introduction

At G-Spirit Loan Solution, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our loan services. By using our services, you agree to the practices described here.

Our Commitment

We're committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how it's used. We follow Cameroonian data protection laws and industry best practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you apply for a loan, we collect:

  • Identity information: full name, date of birth, nationality, and government-issued ID number (National ID, Passport, or Driver's License)
  • Contact information: phone number, WhatsApp number, email address, and physical address
  • Financial information: income details, occupation, bank account, and mobile money account details
  • Collateral information: details of assets offered as collateral (land titles, vehicle papers, etc.)
  • Employment information: occupation, employer details, and proof of income

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we also automatically collect:

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Referring website addresses
  • Cookies and similar technologies

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

  • Payment processors — for fund disbursement and payment collection
  • Verification services — to confirm your identity and collateral
  • Legal and accounting services — as needed for business operations

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information where required by law or in response to valid requests from public authorities.

4.3 Business Transfers

If G-Spirit Loan Solution is ever involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

4.4 With Your Consent

We may share your information for other purposes if you've given us explicit consent to do so.

5. Data Security

We use a range of technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure servers and firewalls
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments
  • Staff training on data protection

While we work hard to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we can't guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as necessary to provide our services, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. As a general rule, we retain loan application data for 5 years after a loan is fully repaid, or 3 years after a rejected application.

7. Your Rights

Under Cameroonian data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal information
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Erasure — request deletion of your information, subject to our legal obligations
  • Restriction — limit how we use your information
  • Objection — object to certain types of processing
  • Portability — receive your information in a structured, transferable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at gloansolution@gmail.com or via WhatsApp at +237 620 569 864.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to remember your preferences, analyze site traffic, improve your experience, and serve relevant offers. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling them may affect how the site functions.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may link to third-party sites (such as Facebook). We aren't responsible for their privacy practices, and we encourage you to review their own policies.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for individuals 21 and older. We don't knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with information, please contact us so we can address it.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than Cameroon. Where this happens, we take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.
