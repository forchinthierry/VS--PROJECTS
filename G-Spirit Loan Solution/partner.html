G-Spirit Loan Solution is opening its doors to a small circle of partners who want their capital working for them — not sitting idle. Every partnership begins with a simple, fixed unit: one Growth Share.

The Growth Share

1 Share = 100,000 XAF

Every new partner joins by acquiring one or more Growth Shares. Your total contribution is simply the number of shares you hold, multiplied by 100,000 XAF. Straightforward, transparent, and easy to track.

How Partnership Works

🌱 Capital at Work Your contribution joins the loan pool that funds applicants — teachers, farmers, small businesses, and more.
📊 Profit Sharing Partners receive a share of net profits generated from interest and fees, proportional to the number of shares held.
🗓️ Payout Schedule Profit distributions are reviewed and paid out on a schedule agreed with each partner — typically quarterly.
🔒 Commitment Period Shares are held for a minimum term before withdrawal to keep the loan pool stable for borrowers.
🚪 Exit & Withdrawal Partners may request to withdraw capital after the minimum term, subject to available liquidity and notice period.
🤝 Silent Partnership Partners contribute capital and share in profits but are not involved in day-to-day lending decisions.
⚠️ Important: Partnership involves real financial risk — profits are not guaranteed and depend on loan repayment performance. Exact profit-sharing percentages, payout timing, and withdrawal terms will be confirmed in a signed partnership agreement before any funds are accepted. We strongly encourage every prospective partner to review the agreement carefully (and seek independent legal or financial advice) before contributing capital.
